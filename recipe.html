BUG PIE

Ingredients needed to make the pie:

  1. 2 - Spiders (prefferably brown recluse)
  2. 1 - cup of dragons blood
  3. 2 - tblsp of ground cockroaches
  4. 3 - tsp of mixed vanilla extract from grasshopper spit.

Take the 1 cup of dragon's blood, both spiders, and the 3 tsp of mixed vanilla extract from the grasshopper spit and place it in a glass mixing bowl. Then, mix with a hand mixer until a firm consistency. Place in the microwave for 20 minutes, letting sit for 10 minutes after done. Using pot holders, remove the finished product from the microwave, cover and place in the fridge for the night. In the morning sprinkle the 2 tblsp of ground cockroaches over the top and serve with a spoon.
